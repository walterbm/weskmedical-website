Wesk Medical is a medical device design company born from the Department of Bioengineering at the University of Maryland.

We work in collaboration with medical professionals and industry partners to design, prototype, and develop unique medical devices that address shortcomings in patient care.

Projects

ETAG CATHETER

Over 50 million intubations are performed annually but no existing medical devices can accurately deliver medicine to the lungs of patients undergoing these procedures.

Wesk Medical has developed the Endotracheal Aerosol Generating (ETAG) catheter, an innovative medical device that promises to improve the efficacy of inhaled drug administration. The ETAG catheter is designed for use by patients undergoing intubation for the treatment of Acute Respiratory Failure.
